What is data science?

"Data science" realized a disciplinary approach to interpreting and analyzing data, both structured and unstructured. To arrive at these conclusions, it relies on techniques such as statistics, mathematics, analytics, programming, and scientific methods. Data science aids in the discovery of trends and patterns in data, allowing stakeholders to make well-informed decisions about their businesses. The data science approach relies on the use of analytical tools and techniques to extract and interpret information. Predictive analysis can be used to help solve complex industrial problems.
